gstaichi.aot
============

.. py:module:: gstaichi.aot

.. autoapi-nested-parse::

   GsTaichi's AOT (ahead of time) module.

   Users can use GsTaichi as a GPU compute shader/kernel compiler by compiling their
   GsTaichi kernels into an AOT module.
